== Description ==

NZ Scene is a travel blog theme which optionally integrates your Instagram feed to provide an attractive and personalized home page banner . Of course you can also add your Insta to the sidebar on any page. The theme offers all the usual customization features including author bios, related posts and the ability to customize your logo, social media links and the basic theme colors.
